# Day 11 Multi-Hop Evaluation Worksheet

This worksheet defines **10 multi-hop queries** designed from real S1000D maintenance scenarios, restricted to the corpus elements in `package-a` and `package-c` (INV-1). 
* **Queries 1–7** are valid multi-hop questions requiring the retrieval engine to link facts across 2 or 3 distinct S1000D Data Modules.
* **Queries 8–10** are **no-answer traps** that violate applicability limits or refer to information not present in the indexed corpus.

| **MH-01** | Multi-Hop (ATA 29) | During hydraulic low system pressure troubleshooting, if the pressure differential indicator is normal but pressure remains low, what part number should you order for replacement, and what support equipment is needed to remove it? | **answer** | `421A` $\rightarrow$ `520A` $\rightarrow$ `941A` | Replace the engine-driven pump. The part number is **`LA-29-4711-1`**. Required support equipment for removal: **5 L drain container** and **10-60 Nm torque wrench**. |
| **MH-02** | Multi-Hop (ATA 29) | If low system pressure troubleshooting leads to replacing the pump, what is the torque requirement for the line fittings when installing the new one, and what precaution must be taken before the first engine run? | **answer** | `421A` $\rightarrow$ `720A` | Torque the supply and pressure line fittings to **`18 Nm`**. Precaution: **Do not operate the pump dry**; fill the pump case with clean hydraulic fluid before the first engine run. |
| **MH-03** | Multi-Hop (ATA 29) | What are the part numbers and torque values for all the fasteners and gaskets used when removing and reinstalling the engine-driven hydraulic pump? | **answer** | `520A` $\rightarrow$ `720A` $\rightarrow$ `941A` | Mounting bolts: P/N **`LA-29-0025-4`**, torqued to **`25 Nm`** in a cross pattern. Pad gasket: P/N **`LA-29-4711-9`** (no torque specified, installed first). Line fittings: torqued to **`18 Nm`**. |
| **MH-04** | Multi-Hop (Cross-ATA) | Before removing the main battery on serial numbers 0001–0050, what preliminary conditions must be met, and how do they compare with the electrical requirements for removing the hydraulic pump? | **answer** | `24-50-520A` $\rightarrow$ `29-10-520A` | Battery removal requires: **Aircraft electrical power is OFF** and **battery switch is tagged out**. Hydraulic pump removal only requires: **Aircraft electrical power is OFF**. Battery removal has the additional tag-out requirement. |
| **MH-05** | Multi-Hop (ATA 29) | Which gasket part number is required to install the hydraulic pump on the accessory gearbox pad, and what supporting equipment is needed for this installation? | **answer** | `720A` $\rightarrow$ `941A` | Gasket part number is **`LA-29-4711-9`**. Supporting equipment needed for installation: **Torque wrench, 10-60 Nm**. |
| **MH-06** | Multi-Hop (ATA 32 $\rightarrow$ 29) | If the main landing gear does not retract and the hydraulic pressure is low, which troubleshooting task should be performed first, and what specific pressure threshold triggers this troubleshooting? | **answer** | `32-10-421A` $\rightarrow$ `29-10-421A` | The **hydraulic low-pressure fault isolation** procedure must be done first. It is triggered when hydraulic system pressure is **below 2400 psi** (with engine at or above 60% N2). |
| **MH-07** | Multi-Hop (ATA 32) | What are the visual inspection steps for the main landing gear shock strut extension, and how does this check relate to troubleshooting a gear that will not retract? | **answer** | `32-10-310A` $\rightarrow$ `32-10-421A` | Check the shock strut for fluid leakage and correct extension (**55-65 mm of visible chrome**). Both cover main landing gear (ATA 32), but shock strut checks are part of routine inspections and are not listed in the gear-non-retraction isolation steps. |

| **MH-09** | Trap (Applicability) | What is the disconnect sequence for the main battery on airframes with serial number 0080? | **refuse** | `{}` | **Out-of-corpus / Refuse**. `24-50-00-00A-520A-A` applicability is restricted to **serial numbers 0001–0050**. There is no battery removal procedure for serial number 0080 in the corpus. |
| **MH-10** | Trap (Missing Attribute) | During hydraulic low pressure fault isolation, if the pressure filter differential indicator is extended, what is the part number of the replacement filter element? | **refuse** | `{}` | **Out-of-corpus / Refuse**.
